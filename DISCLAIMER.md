**Last Updated**: 25 March 2026

## 1. GroPesa (GRO) Token
- **Not an Investment**: GRO is a **utility token** for redeeming airtime/data. It is **not a security or investment**.
- **No Value Guarantee**: The value of GRO may fluctuate or drop to zero. We do not guarantee its price.
- **Irreversible Transactions**: GRO transactions **cannot be reversed**. Double-check wallet addresses.
- **Regulatory Risks**: GRO’s legality depends on your jurisdiction. In Kenya, it complies with **CBK sandbox regulations**.

## 2. Mesh Network
- **Offline Limitations**: Messages may fail if:
  - Devices are **out of range** (~100m for Wi-Fi Direct).
  - **Interference** disrupts the signal (e.g., thick walls).
  - **Battery saving modes** disable Wi-Fi Direct.
- **No Internet Backup**: If the mesh fails, messages **won’t deliver** until devices reconnect.
- **Super-Node Dependency**: PCs acting as relayers may **go offline**.

## 3. Security
- **E2E Encryption**: Messages are encrypted, but **metadata** (e.g., timestamps) is visible to peers.
- **KYC Data**: National IDs are **deleted after verification** but may be shared with **FaceIO** for liveness checks.
- **Smart Contract Risks**: While audited (Slither/MythX), bugs may exist. Use at your own risk.
